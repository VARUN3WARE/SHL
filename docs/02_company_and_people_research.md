# 02 - SHL Company and People Research

## Company snapshot

SHL is a talent assessment and talent insight company focused on hiring, development, mobility, workforce skills, and people analytics. Its public positioning is "People Science. People Answers." The company emphasizes science-backed assessment, large-scale workforce data, and technology-enabled talent decisions.

Useful public facts from SHL's site:

- SHL says it serves over 10,000 businesses worldwide.
- SHL says about 35 million assessments are taken every year.
- SHL says its science draws on over 45 billion talent-data points.
- Its product range includes cognitive assessments, personality and behavior assessments, skills assessments, coding/language simulations, 360 feedback, video interviews, and assessment/development centers.

Sources:

- https://www.shl.com/
- https://www.shl.com/products/
- https://www.shl.com/solutions/products/assessments/

## Why this assignment fits SHL

The task is not just a generic RAG chatbot. It mirrors real SHL product problems:

- recruiters often describe roles informally instead of using the right assessment taxonomy;
- SHL has a large catalog with overlapping skill, cognitive, personality, simulation, and report products;
- assessment selection needs high precision because recommendations influence real hiring workflows;
- SHL cares about defensible, grounded people-science decisions, so hallucinations are especially damaging.

## Product context useful for the assignment

SHL's assessment product pages describe several major assessment types:

- Cognitive assessments: Verify G+, deductive, inductive, numerical reasoning.
- Personality assessments: OPQ/OPQ32r.
- Skills and simulations: technical, functional, coding, and job-related skills assessments.
- Global Skills Assessment: measures 96 behavioral skills aligned to SHL's Universal Competency Framework.

Example catalog facts:

- `Java 8 (New)` is a knowledge/skills test covering Java class design, exceptions, generics, collections, concurrency, JDBC, and Java I/O fundamentals. It is listed as 18 minutes and test type `K`.
- `Occupational Personality Questionnaire OPQ32r` is a personality/behavior assessment, untimed around 25 minutes, test type `P`.
- `SHL Verify Interactive G+` measures general cognitive ability plus deductive, inductive, and numerical reasoning, listed as 36 minutes and test type `A`.
- `Global Skills Assessment` measures 96 discrete skills/behaviors aligned to the Universal Competency Framework, listed as 16 minutes and test type `C K`.

## How to use this company research in the submission

Do not overdo company flattery in the approach document. Use the context to make better design choices:

- emphasize grounded recommendations and catalog-only URLs;
- frame the agent as an assessment-selection assistant, not a general HR advisor;
- mention fairness/defensibility by explaining refusal, grounding, and validation;
- discuss empirical evaluation because SHL's culture values science and measurement.
